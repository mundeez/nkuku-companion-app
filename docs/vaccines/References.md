## 10. Confidence Levels Used in This Guide

- **Low / Local practice:** Common field practice or farmer-reported approach that may not be universally evidence-based; flagged as such in the main guide.
- **Not verified:** Where specific local data could not be confirmed, this is explicitly stated rather than assumed.
